About

I am Nigerian-American 2nd year PhD student from Austin, TX. Graduated from the University of Texas at Dallas in December of 2021 and began working as a software developer for PwC in Dallas, TX shortly after. In 2022 I worked with IBM Research Tech4Justice team, in Yorktown, New York, on creating a more automous and systematic way for evaluating Large Langue Models such as BERT and GPT3. Currently I am advised by Dr. Vladmir Braverman, with goals to utilize machine learning algorithms to solve healthcare related problems. I love traveling and eating all kinds of cuisines, and learning about new technologies.
